Privacy Policy

Food Trend Analyzer | Last updated: May 29, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Food Trend Analyzer ("the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our service. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

The App accesses the following data through TikTok's official Research API:

  • Publicly available video metadata (view counts, like counts, hashtags)
  • Aggregate trend data for food-related content
  • Publicly visible creator profile information (username, follower counts)

We do not collect private messages, personal contact information, or any data not publicly available through TikTok's authorized API endpoints.

3. How We Use Information

Data collected through the TikTok Research API is used exclusively for:

  • Analyzing food content trends in Thailand
  • Generating market research insights
  • Internal analytics and reporting

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Trend data is processed and stored securely. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. TikTok Data Compliance

Our use of TikTok data complies fully with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service and Research API usage policies. Data is accessed only through authorized API endpoints and used solely for stated research purposes.

6. Data Retention

Aggregated trend data is retained for up to 12 months for research analysis purposes. No personally identifiable information is retained beyond what is necessary for the research.

7. Your Rights

If you have concerns about data related to your TikTok content being included in our research analysis, you may contact us at the email below. We will review and address your request in accordance with applicable law.
